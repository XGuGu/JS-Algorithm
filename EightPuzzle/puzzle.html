8 Puzzle Solver

The Problem

The 8 puzzle problem is a puzzle invented and popularized by Noyes Palmer Chapman in the 1870s. It is played on a 3-by-3 grid with 8 square puzzle labeled 1 through 8 and a blank space. The player is permitted to slide puzzle horizontally or vertically into the blank space.
